About

TaroHacks is an online hackathon for high school students in grades 9-12 taking place from August 1st to August 8th, 2020. During this event you'll be able to work on a project to help solve a problem or human need,and pitch it to the judges for a chance to win some prizes. Although normally hackathons are daylong, in-real-life events, COVID-19 has made this difficult to do, so with this hackathon students will be able to use their skills to create something to benefit society and be recognized for their work! This is the first year this hackathon will be run, started by a Bay Area robotics team, FTC Team Taro. We are a seven member team that aims to spread stem and robotics to people around the world!

Competition Details

All of the information will be sent through email and hackathon discord to the participants during and before the hackathon. Workshops, speakers, and general meetings will be hosted through Zoom. Entries will be submitted through the our Devpost page, and will be judged the hours after submissions are due.
